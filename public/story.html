Who we are

" Do Things That Matters. Plan. Create. Grow."

I’ve always been passionate about brands as a kid. What school did not teach me but summer vacations definitely did. Schools' summer holidays were really special back then from visiting nani’s place at marine drive and renting a cycle of 20 rupees For one hour with my cousin and a ride to the queen's necklace while experiencing scenic views, beautiful hotels & people.

As an enthusiastic kid, one fine day I got a chance to visit my uncle’s workplace while experiencing learning lessons of the sales process in the retail sector of Mumbai's renowned sports store. In 2012, IPL season was in full swing the store used to be crowded with customers. A young lady was looking for bunch of mumbai indian’s jersey it was very first time i experienced sales and assisted her with all her requirements and queries and communicated smoothly, After the billing was done the customer was really happy and satisfied with service she said to me ‘’Kevin, thank you so much and good job! ‘’ gave me a 100 rupees note as a tip while I was astonished as a teenage kid knowing she was impressed by my salesmanship. There she ignited a spark within me to dream big and to achieve it!

I decided to pursue my career into management studies and entrepreneurship while experiencing internship programmes during my graduation. One night, i just completed one of my college projects and a thought came up to start something with CORPORATE focusing on empowerment of entrepreneurship and corporate culture to college students. I pitched my idea to college authorities and then we came up with CIIE (Centre for Incubation, Innovation & Entrepreneurship) To connect with industry insight from business point of view to spiritually from stalwarts and renowned personalities we organised 4 big events

  • Start-up Summit 2019 ( Riyaz & Reshma Gangji, Devesh Chawla,Gaurav Singh)
  • Spiritual Success with Chaitanya Lila Das (Iskcon chowpatty)
  • Meet the author with Anirban Das (IIM Ahmedabad)
  • Pitching sessions with Navayuvak Entrepreneurs ( Gaurav Mishra)

I’d appreciate the support of Dr. Lilly Bhushan, Prof. Sweta Mishra, Prof. Jaanvi Joshi, Prof Vaibhav Ashar. Special thanks to Atman Shastri and team for assisting to make all these sessions a great success! I was awarded as secretary of CIIE 19-20 & Library advisory committee 19-20 I’d like to announce the launch of Corporatecube - Design thinking & ideas With bigger & better vision.
